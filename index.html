Our Story

Little Bites is a bakery shop located in the Malejor neighborhood of Adenta Dodowa Road. Serving sourdough breads, pies, conchas, and a variety of croissants, we use French baking techniques and locally inspired flavors to create a rotating menu of handcrafted baked goods. The menu also features many plant-based options.
